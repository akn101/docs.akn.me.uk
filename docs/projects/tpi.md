# The Pathway Initiative (TPI) — tpi.akn.org.uk

**URL:** [https://tpi.akn.org.uk](https://tpi.akn.org.uk)
**Type:** Educational charity / volunteer tutoring service
**Founded:** 2024
**Contact:** pathway.projects@akn.org.uk

---

## Overview

**The Pathway Initiative (TPI)** is a volunteer-run online tutoring organisation founded in 2024, dedicated to helping students from diverse backgrounds achieve academic success through high-quality, personalised instruction.

Their headline mission: **"Potential is everywhere."**

TPI provides free or accessible tutoring across a broad range of subjects, delivered by a team of volunteer educators who are passionate about helping students reach their full potential.

---

## Subjects Offered

TPI provides tutoring in the following subjects:

| Subject | Notes |
|---------|-------|
| **Mathematics** | Foundational concepts and problem-solving; all tutors have a strong Maths foundation |
| **Chemistry** | Organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry; focus on practical applications and exam readiness |
| **English** | Taught by native English speakers; includes ESL (English as a Second Language) |
| **French** | Native/near-native speaker instruction |
| **Spanish** | Native/near-native speaker instruction |
| **German** | Modern foreign language tutoring |
| **Italian** | Modern foreign language tutoring |

> **Note:** A key requirement of TPI is that **all tutors teach and have a strong foundation in Maths**, regardless of their primary subject specialism.

---

## Key Features

### Personalised Learning Plans
- Each student receives a **customised learning plan** tailored to their individual needs, strengths, and target goals
- Sessions are not one-size-fits-all; tutors adapt to the student's pace and learning style

### Proven Results
- Teaching uses **evidence-based methodologies** with documented track records of improving grades and test scores
- Progress is tracked and reported to students and parents

### Flexible Scheduling
- Sessions available **after school, on weekends, and during school holidays**
- Scheduling accommodates students' existing commitments

### Qualified, Friendly Tutors
- Volunteer tutors who are genuinely passionate about education
- Native speakers for all modern foreign language (MFL) subjects
- Supportive, encouraging teaching style

## How to Apply

Students can apply for tutoring through the TPI website:

1. Visit [tpi.akn.org.uk](https://tpi.akn.org.uk) and navigate to **Apply**
2. Select your desired subject(s) from the dropdown
3. Fill in the contact form with your name, email, and message
4. Submit — a tutor will be in touch to discuss your needs

Subject-specific application pathways are available for each discipline.
