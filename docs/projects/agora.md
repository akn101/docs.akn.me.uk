## Overview

**Agora** is a registered UK charity that builds and operates a **secure, community-driven mobile platform designed to help refugees overcome social isolation** by connecting them with others who share their cultural, linguistic, and regional backgrounds.

The organisation's mission is captured in its tagline:

> "A world where no refugee has to feel alone."

Agora empowers users to build localised digital communities while maintaining autonomy and strong privacy protections.

---

## The Problem

Research underpinning Agora's founding highlights the scale of the issue:

- **Over two-thirds** of refugees report significant social isolation upon arriving in the UK
- **Half** feel like outsiders even after settling
- Many struggle to find peers from the same culture, language group, or region
- Existing support services are widely considered inadequate for addressing social connection

---

## The Solution — The Agora Platform

The Agora mobile app addresses refugee isolation through six core feature areas:

### 1. Group Discovery
Users can filter and discover groups by:
- **Identity** — cultural or ethnic background
- **Language** — shared native or spoken language
- **Location** — geographic proximity within the UK

### 2. Secure Chat
- End-to-end encrypted messaging
- Private and group chat rooms
- Safe, confidential communication

### 3. Safety First
The platform prioritises user safety with multiple layers of protection:
- **Account verification** to reduce fake accounts
- **AI-powered content moderation** to detect and remove harmful content
- **Panic buttons** for users who feel unsafe

### 4. Event Hosting
- Users and groups can organise **real-world meetups** and events
- Tools for scheduling, RSVPs, and event discovery within the platform

### 5. Multilingual Support
- The platform is available in multiple languages
- Enables refugees who are not yet fluent in English to access all features

### 6. Privacy Protection
- **Minimal data collection** — the platform collects only what is necessary
- **Non-precise location** — location is used to connect people in the same area without revealing exact addresses
- Users retain control over their personal data
