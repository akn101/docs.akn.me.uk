## Overview

**AuraCare** is a digital pharmacy management and triage platform designed to **connect pharmacists with NHS systems and provide AI-powered diagnostics**.

It streamlines patient care workflows by providing a unified dashboard for pharmacists to assess symptoms, manage patients, coordinate with medical teams, and track operations — all from a single interface.

The platform currently operates in **Demo Mode**, with a sample pharmacist identity and pharmacy reference (`Pharmacy #12345`).

---

## Core Features

### 1. AI-Powered Symptom Analysis
- Pharmacists can enter patient symptoms and receive **AI-driven diagnostic assessments**
- Designed to support — not replace — clinical judgement
- Helps triage patients quickly and recommend appropriate next steps (e.g. refer to GP, dispense OTC medication, call 999)

### 2. Patient Management
- Centralised **patient database** for registered pharmacy patients
- Patient records accessible from the dashboard
- Track patient history, previous consultations, and ongoing issues

### 3. Medical Team Coordination
- Interface for contacting and coordinating with the wider **NHS medical team**
- Enables pharmacists to escalate cases to GPs, nurses, or specialists
- Facilitates joined-up care across healthcare providers

### 4. Ticket System
- An internal **task and issue tracking system** for pharmacy operations
- Raise, assign, and resolve operational tickets (e.g. supply issues, system faults, patient complaints)
- Provides audit trails for accountability

### 5. Real-Time Chat
- Built-in **team messaging** for quick internal communication
- Enables pharmacists to consult colleagues or reach the medical team in real-time
- Reduces reliance on phone calls or external messaging apps

### 6. Inventory Management
- Track **stock levels** of medicines and pharmacy supplies
- Receive alerts for low stock or expiry approaching
- Manage orders and supply chain information

### 7. Analytics Dashboard
- Performance metrics for the pharmacy (consultations, tickets, patient volume)
- Trends over time to support operational decision-making
- Reports that can be exported for management review

### 8. Integrations
- Settings panel for connecting external systems (e.g. NHS Spine, EHR platforms, prescription services)
- Designed to slot into existing NHS IT infrastructure

## UI & Design

- Supports **light and dark mode** switching via a theme toggle
- Responsive design for both **desktop and mobile** devices
- Designed for use in fast-paced pharmacy environments — clear, minimal, and accessible

## Demo Mode

The current deployment at `auracare.dev.auracle.akn.org.uk` runs in **Demo Mode**:

- No real patient data is used
- A sample pharmacist identity is loaded by default
- Pharmacy is identified as **#12345**
- Useful for evaluating the platform or onboarding new team members
